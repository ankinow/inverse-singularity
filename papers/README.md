## Related Work

The following bodies of work are relevant to IST:

- **Scaling laws** (Kaplan et al., Hoffmann et al.) — The orthodoxy IST inverts: more compute → diminishing returns.
- **Constraint satisfaction** (Mackworth, Freuder) — Constraint as a computational framework, predating IST's constraint-as-catalyst thesis.
- **OODA Loop** (John Boyd) — Observe-Orient-Decide-Act cycle. The ancestor of IST's collapse mode: compressed decision cycles under time pressure.
- **Information theory** (Shannon) — Entropy, signal-to-noise ratio. Direct mathematical ancestry for $\phi(d)/\kappa(M)$.
- **Grothendieck's relative point of view** — Properties defined not absolutely but relative to a base. Parallels the sovereign invariant: quality is undefined without boundary.
